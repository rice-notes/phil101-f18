Moral Theories

A moral theory is a framework from which to consider moral issues. It's how a philosopher might approach a rising issue, or argue a viewpoint on a specific one.

What's the point?

  • Allows you to consistently argue from a specific viewpoint
  • Allows two philosophers to collaborate on an argument from a consistent view
