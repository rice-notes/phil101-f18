Marquis

Assumptions:

  • fetuses in same category as human beings w.r.t. moral worth, then abortion is on par with killing of adults

Why Killing is Wrong

Marquis argues that we need to understand and analyze why killing is wrong before we get into why abortion is wrong. His main point is that the loss of a life deprives one of all potential goods of their life, such as their experiences, dreams, and knowledge, etc... We can compare this with the death of animals, in which case they don't have experiences, dreams, or hopes. Basically, death is the largest difference of realized and potential worth of a person, and the largest that difference can be is when death comes before birth.
